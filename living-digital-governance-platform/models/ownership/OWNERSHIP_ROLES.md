# Ownership Roles

## Owner
The person or function accountable for the existence, purpose, quality, and lifecycle of a governance object.

## Steward
The person or function responsible for maintaining metadata, documentation, quality controls, and operational follow-up.

## Reviewer
The person or function responsible for checking consistency, completeness, risk, and compliance before approval.

## Approver
The person or authority allowed to approve controlled use of a governance object.

## Accountable Authority
The institutional authority ultimately accountable for sensitive or strategic governance decisions.

## Human Validator
The human role required to validate AI outputs before operational, legal, institutional, or sensitive use.
