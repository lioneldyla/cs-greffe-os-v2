# Validation Rules

Core validation rules:

- No critical object can become Active without validation.
- AI outputs require human validation before operational or sensitive use.
- Decisions require accountable authority validation.
- High-risk objects require enhanced review.
- Validation must be traceable.
- Validation must identify validator, date, object, scope, and decision.
- Rejected objects must include a reason.
- Archived objects remain traceable.
